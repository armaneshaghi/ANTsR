=========================
get-mask 
=========================

purpose:  

 Get Mask
 
description:  

 Get a mask-image from the given image after thresholding.
 

usage:  

 get_mask( img , thresh_lo , thresh_hi )
 

.. sourcecode:: r
    

    examples:
    
    # input_img.nii is a 3D image
    
    mask_img <- get_mask("input_img.nii", 500, 1e+09)
