=========================
getROIValue 
=========================

purpose:  

 Compute mean value in each ROI label.
 
description:  

 Computes the mean value in each label of an ROI after masking with MaskImage
 

usage:  

 wmat<-getROIValue( valueImage, ROIImage, maskImage  )
 
examples: 

.. sourcecode:: r
    

    vals <- getROIValue(image, aal, mask)
